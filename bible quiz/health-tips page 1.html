Health benefits of some fruits

Orange
Oranges are sources of vitamin c, contain vitamin B1, folate, and potassium which are useful for boosting the bodies immunity.

Strawberry
Strawberries contain vitamin c and are rich in antioxidants, which are helpful in reducing the growth of cancerous cells.

Avocado
Avocados are filled with loads of vitamin E. Giving your skin a radiant youthful glow. They facilitate blood flow to the brain keeping
you alert and focused.
